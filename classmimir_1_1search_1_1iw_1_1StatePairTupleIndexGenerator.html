Detailed Description

StatePairTupleIndexGenerator encapsulates iterator logic to generate all combinations of tuple indices of size at most the arity such that at least one atom index is coming from add_atom_indices and the others from atom_indices.

The underlying algorithm is an adaption of std::next_permutation with constant amortized cost to compute the next tuple index.
