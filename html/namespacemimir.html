class  StaticGraph
 StaticGraph G implements a static version of a directed graph with vertices of type V and edges of type E satisfying the graph concepts: 1) VertexListGraph, 2) EdgeListGraph, 3) IncidenceGraph, and 4) AdjacencyGraph. The meaning of static is that the graph allows for the addition but not for the removal of vertices and edges. Due to the restriction to addition functionality, the implementation uses more efficient vector data structures compared to DynamicGraph. More...
