Detailed Description

template<IsVertex V, IsEdge E>
class mimir::graphs::DynamicGraph< V, E >

DynamicGraph implements a directed graph with vertices of type V and edges of type E that supports the insertion and deletion of vertices and edges satisfying the graph concepts: IsVertexListGraph, IsEdgeListGraph, IsIncidenceGraph, IsAdjacencyGraph, and IsBidirectionalGraph.

DynamicGraph supports efficient traversal of adjacent vertices and edges in forward and backward directions.

Eis the edge type.
