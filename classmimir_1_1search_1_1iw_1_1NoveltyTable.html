Detailed Description

template<StaticOrDynamicSize Mode>
class mimir::search::iw::NoveltyTable< Mode >

NoveltyTable encapsulates a table to test novelty of tuples of atoms of size at most arity. It automatically resizes when the atoms do not fit into the table anymore.
