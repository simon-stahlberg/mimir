class  mimir::StaticGraph< V, E >
 StaticGraph G implements a static version of a directed graph with vertices of type V and edges of type E satisfying the graph concepts: 1) VertexListGraph, 2) EdgeListGraph, 3) IncidenceGraph, and 4) AdjacencyGraph. The meaning of static is that the graph allows for the addition but not for the removal of vertices and edges.
